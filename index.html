About Me

I am a first-year computer science major at Boston University, but I have been passionate about technology for as long as I can remember. Over the past five years I have enjoyed opportunities teaching children to code, researching cutting-edge technology at Harvard, and engineering personal DIY gadgets. These days I am working as the programming leader for a non-profit organization, the Fenway Victory Gardens.
